About me

Research

Research interests

I work on complex algebraic geometry with a focus on moduli spaces and geometric representation theory. More concretely, my mathematical interests (i.e. things I have been learning, thinking about or that I would like to learn) include:

  • Moduli of quiver representations and quiver bundles.
  • Vector bundles, Higgs bundles and their moduli.
  • Non-abelian Hodge Theory.
  • Construction of moduli spaces via gauge theory and GIT.
  • Stacky and arithmetic versions of the above.
